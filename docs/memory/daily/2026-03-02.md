## Billing note: Browser Rendering vs AI Gateway

**Browser Rendering (MYBROWSER / Puppeteer)** is **not** part of your AI Gateway budget. It’s a separate Cloudflare product with its own billing:

- **Product:** [Cloudflare Browser Rendering](https://developers.cloudflare.com/browser-rendering/platform/pricing) (headless Chrome at the edge).
- **How you’re using it:** Workers Bindings (`MYBROWSER` in `wrangler.production.toml`); used by `/api/browser/health`, `/api/browser/metrics`, and the queue consumer for screenshot/render jobs.
- **Billing:** On **Workers Paid**: 10 browser hours/month included, then **$0.09 per additional browser hour**; 10 concurrent browsers included (averaged monthly), then **$2.00 per additional concurrent browser**. Free plan: 10 minutes of browser time per day. Usage appears on your **Cloudflare Workers / account** bill, not as “AI Gateway.”
- **AI Gateway** is a different product (for proxying/routing to OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.). LLM API costs (e.g. Claude, GPT) are from the provider or your AI Gateway config, not from Browser Rendering.

So: Browser Rendering usage will show up as its own line (e.g. “Browser Rendering” or under Workers usage) in the Cloudflare dashboard, not under AI Gateway.
